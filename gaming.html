Clubs at Burnside

Why should I join a Gaming club?

Getting involved in the school gaming club is a great chance to meet other students who are as passionate about gaming as you are. It provides a social setting for strategy discussions, cooperative team games, and friendly tournaments, all of which promote a feeling of community. Because many of the club's games call for strategy and cooperation, it can also aid in the development of critical thinking and problem-solving abilities. It's also a fantastic chance to experiment with various gaming genres and even take part in artistic endeavors like narrative or game design. Whether you're an avid or casual player, the gaming club offers an enjoyable and welcoming atmosphere to improve your abilities and create lifelong friendships!
